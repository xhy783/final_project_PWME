About US

  • About SmartHome

  • SmartHome is an advanced smart home platform designed to provide users with a comprehensive home automation experience. On this platform, users can easily control home temperature, lighting, and other smart devices, creating a more comfortable and efficient lifestyle.

  • Multi-Platform Clients

  • We have developed clients for Web, Android, and iOS. Although the iOS application is currently not available on the App Store, users can still access and use it through other means. These clients are designed to offer an intuitive and smooth user interface, making remote home management simple and convenient.

  • Compatibility with Various IoT Devices

  • The SmartHome platform supports a wide range of IoT devices, which can be connected via Arduino, ensuring seamless integration of smart home products from various brands and models. Our system not only facilitates device control but also provides real-time data connectivity to servers for data analysis and device management.

  • User-Centric Design Philosophy

  • Our team firmly believes that technology should serve people, and therefore, in designing SmartHome, we have always prioritized user experience. From interface design to functionality, we strive for simplicity and ease of use, ensuring that every user can effortlessly enjoy the conveniences of a smart home.

  • Join SmartHome to experience the revolution of smart living, and embark on a new chapter of intelligent, convenient, and efficient lifestyle.
